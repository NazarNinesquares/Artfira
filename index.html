ArtFira Gallery presents Eastern European Art with an emphasis on traditional academic as well as contemporary art.

Represented artists are distinguished by their exceptional skills, strong academic background, personal styles and techniques.

ArtFira Gallery LLC has been founded in 2003. Formerly located at 40 W 23rd Street in New York, we have been operating online and by appointment only over the last 10 years. We have also focused our efforts on researching, collecting and preserving the legacy of a number of immigrant artists of Ukrainian and Russian descend who worked and lived in Venezuela, France, Germany and the USA, among other places.
